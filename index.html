Why GreenPlate

Every Plate Saved
Makes a Difference

Food waste costs Indian colleges ₹2–4 lakh per month. GreenPlate cuts that by predicting demand and redistributing surplus.

🧠
AI Demand Prediction

Our model analyses past consumption patterns, events, and day-of-week trends to forecast how many plates will be needed tomorrow.

♻️
Zero-Waste Distribution

Surplus food is instantly listed for students to reserve at discounted rates, and NGOs to collect — nothing goes to the bin.

💰
Cost Savings

Canteens cook only what's needed. Admin dashboards show daily savings so you can measure ROI in real time.

📊
Live Analytics

Visual dashboards track waste trends, donation volumes, and efficiency scores — making sustainability measurable and motivating.

🤝
NGO Integration

Registered NGOs get instant notifications, can request pickups, and confirm collections — all from a simple dashboard.

🌱
Carbon Impact

Every kilogram of food saved prevents 2.5kg of CO₂ emissions. GreenPlate calculates and displays your campus's environmental impact.

How It Works

From Plate to Purpose
in 4 Steps

A seamless closed-loop system that turns canteen data into action.

01
Admin Logs Data

Canteen staff enter menu, meals prepared, meals sold, and leftover quantities each day.

02
AI Predicts Demand

The system calculates tomorrow's predicted requirement to prevent over-cooking.

03
Surplus Published

Leftover food is published in real time for students to reserve and NGOs to claim.

04
Collection Confirmed

Students pick up reserved meals; NGOs confirm bulk collections. Zero waste achieved.
